## About me

I am a Guatemalan biologist and [entomologist](https://ksorellana.github.io/entomology), currently pursuing a PhD in Evolutionary Biology at [Arizona State University](https://isearch.asu.edu/profile/3433157). My current research focuses on the diversity and systematics of the "fungus weevils", the family [Anthribidae](https://ksorellana.github.io/anthribidae) (Coleoptera: Curculionoidea). During this time, I am also digitizing and imaging the specimens deposited at the [ASU Biocollections](https://biokic.asu.edu/collections) so more researchers can have access to the information. You can find the data and images of the specimens on [ecdysis](https://serv.biokic.asu.edu/ecdysis/index.php). 

As a part of the [Symbiota Support Hub of iDigBio](https://www.idigbio.org/contact/Samanta_Orellana), I am helping to expand the activities of digitization to several countries in Latin America, with the [Symbiota portals](https://symbiota.org/). I lead the [Guatemala Symbiota Portal](https://biodiversidad.gt/portal/), where curators and researchers in my home country are digitizing the local collections.
